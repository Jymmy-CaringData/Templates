6. CARE PLAN:

A written hospice care plan which specifies the care, services, and necessary medical intervention related to the terminal illness will be provided by hospice company to Mom & Dad’s House as necessary to supplement the care and supervision provided by Mom & Dad’s House to ________{{data.resident_name}}________ (Terminally Ill Resident).

The hospice agency and the resident or prospective resident agree to provide the licensee with all information necessary to allow the licensee to comply with all regulations and to assure that the resident's needs will be met.

A current and complete hospice care plan shall be provided by Hospice Company to Mom & Dad’s House for each hospice resident and include the following:

  1. The name, office address, business telephone number, and 24-hour emergency telephone number of the hospice agency and the resident's physician.
  2. A description of the services to be provided in the facility by the hospice agency including but not limited to the type and frequency of services to be provided.
  3. Designation of the resident's primary contact person at the hospice agency.
  4. A description of the area of Mom & Dad’s House responsibility for implementing the plan including, but not limited to, facility staff duties; record keeping; and communication with the hospice agency, resident's physician, and the resident's responsible person(s), if any. This description shall include the type and frequency of the tasks to be performed by Mom & Dad’s House. The plan shall neither require nor recommend that the licensee or any facility personnel other than a physician or appropriately skilled professional implement any health care procedure which may legally be provided only by a physician or appropriately skilled professional.
  5. A description of all hospice services to be provided or arranged in Mom & Dad’s House by persons other licensee than the, facility personnel, or the hospice agency including, but not limited to, clergy and the resident's family members and friends.

9. COMMUNICATIONS:

As part of our ongoing process to streamline communications between Mom & Dad’s House and our providers, Mom & Dad's House has started implementing a digital Provider Report through Caring Data LLC.

Caring Data Provider Report Process

  1. Every provider rendering services to a Mom & Dad’s House resident will be required to setup a new account through Caring Data, LLC. Our staff will give the provider access to the system on our tablets to create an account.
  2. By the end of every visit, providers will sign-in onto their account and fill out a Provider Report. This documentation can be done on M&D House tablets/computers. Before the visit is complete staff will need to see the completed providers report submitted.
  3. The information in the Provider Report will be automatically integrated into the resident’s notes/charting for the staff to view and follow specific instructions.
  4. This report/information will be shared with the resident’s POA when necessary.

For the safety of our resident’s information, Caring Data, LLC, is a HIPAA compliant system.

All visiting house providers will create sign in/out username and password using Caring Data software to fill out a providers report on a smart device/tablet. Please set aside time for providers to be able to submit a Caring Data provides report in appointment scheduling.

Before entering, the following questions will be required to be answered using our Caring Data tracking system.

  • Do you have cough of flu symptoms?
  • Have you been near someone who was sick with COVID-19 in the last 14 days?
  • If visitor has answered YES to any of these we are not able to let you enter.
